Guacamole Recipe

Guacamole

Equal parts tasty and creamy, this is
what millenials forego homeloans for.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados - peeled, pitted
    and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Â½ cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh
    cilantro
  • 2 roma tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper

The Process

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together
    the avocados, lime juice, and salt.
  2. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes,
    and garlic.
  3. Stir in the cayenne pepper.
  4. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor,
    or serve immediately (we know
    you want to).
