Hummus Recipe

Hummus

A personal favorite, hummus is a
cheap and tasty treat. Go ahead and
bust out those chickpeas from the
dusty corner of your pantry. No regrets.

Ingredients

  • 1 dusty can of chickpeas
  • ⅓ cup canned jalapeno pepper
    slices, juice reserved
  • 3 tablespoons tahini
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • ½ teaspoon ground cumin
  • ½ teaspoon curry powder
  • crushed red pepper to taste

The Process

  1. Blend your chickpeas,
    jalapeno peppers w/juice,
    tahini, garlic, and lemon juice.
  2. Season with cumin, curry powder,
    and crushed red peppers to taste.
  3. Blend until Smooth.
  4. Garnish it with herbs you
    stole from your Mum's garden.
